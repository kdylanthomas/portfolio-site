About Me

I spent the beginning of my career in fields where I could analyze data to paint a bigger picture, from performing volcanic research as a geologist to crunching numbers in the world of business technology. Still, nothing felt like a perfect fit until I discovered coding through coworkers and friends. As soon as I started learning on my own, I was in love. I decided to attend Nashville Software School to jumpstart my programming education in a place where I could work with like-minded people and learn best practices. Over the last six months, I’ve been rapidly building my skills as a full-stack developer and getting more excited every day about learning new technologies and finding ways to improve my code.

I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments where I can work on teams with fun-yet-hardworking people to build creative solutions and create technology that makes an impact. When I’m not building applications at school or tinkering with personal projects, you can probably find me outside, whether I’m rock climbing, whitewater paddling, or just lounging in my hammock.

Bonnawho?

Front-end capstone

Bonnawho? is your pocket guide to the Bonnaroo 2016 lineup. Through the app, users can view the lineup through convenient filters and save artists to listen to later. The app integrates with the Spotify API to let users learn more about their saved artists, including access to the artist’s recorded albums and top songs. Users can rate and comment on their saved artists, and the app uses that information to build a suggested schedule for the user.
